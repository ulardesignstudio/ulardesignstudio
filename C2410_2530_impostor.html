Portfolio

The Impostor project involved the comprehensive design of a boutique, small-scale restaurant situated in Lourinhã, Portugal.

The primary architectural challenge was to optimise the seating capacity within a compact footprint, ensuring maximum functional efficiency and fluid circulation for both staff and guests.

The visual identity was meticulously curated to strike a balance between a relaxed, laid-back atmosphere and refined classic elements, resulting in a dining environment that is both timeless and welcoming.
