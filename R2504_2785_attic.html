The Concept
This project involved the transformation of an existing attic into a fully functional living space, with a focus on the development of the main common areas, including the living room, kitchen and dining area.

Design & Layout
The intervention included the design of open-plan layouts, the introduction of large windows to enhance natural light, and the development of the outdoor patio, supported by 3D visualisations to communicate the proposed design and overall spatial outcome.
