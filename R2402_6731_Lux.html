The Brief
In this project, the clients sought to renovate their living room by optimising existing elements, such as the sofa and tables. This approach was driven by a commitment to both budget efficiency and environmental sustainability.

Design & Personality
New elements were carefully curated to reflect the clients' character: a young, fun, yet elegant couple. The introduction of bespoke wallpaper and a dynamic gallery wall added layers of texture and sophistication to the space.

The Transformation
By focusing on these thoughtful details and the strategic interplay of decorative pieces, it was possible to completely transform the atmosphere of the living room while honouring its original foundation.
