The Morgado project consists of a premium, full-service interior design development for a two-bedroom apartment and its private garden, tailored for a young couple.

Our intervention covered every stage of the process, from initial layout optimization to the complete interior decoration of the kitchen, living room, bedroom, closet, and bathrooms, including the outdoor area.

The project involved the creation of detailed moodboards, extensive furniture research, and personalized guidance in every selection to ensure a cohesive and high-end result.

This project involved the spatial redesign of a 56m apartment, transforming it into a modern, open-concept. The intervention merged the kitchen and living area into a single 36m social space, complemented by two 11m bedrooms and a 4m bathroom.

The standout feature is the seamless connection to the 32m terrace; the installation of expansive new windows effectively 'doubles' the home's feel, blurring the line between interior and exterior.
